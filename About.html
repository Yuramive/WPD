About Us


The "Sexy Pitches" was founded in 2014. As New Paltz's only all-girl Acapella group, We pride ourselves on our custom- made arrangements and student run events. The Sexy Pitches participate in yearly local and state wide competitions, and has attended and competed in several National competitions!
